# Complete User Journeys - Sarkin Mota Autos

This document maps out the specific user archetypes, their goals, entry points, actions, pain points, conversion paths, and desired outcomes on the Sarkin Mota Autos platform.

---

## 1. Anonymous Visitor (The Casual / Browser)
*   **Goal**: View high-end exotic cars, read reviews, or play with the AI car match tool.
*   **Entry Points**: Social media links, brand search, political candidate mentions.
*   **Actions**:
    *   Scroll through the homepage carousel.
    *   Click vehicle links or check the `/news` portal.
    *   Initiate chat with `MyBratha` AI widget out of curiosity.
*   **Pain Points**: Cluttered interfaces, high load times for large video assets, lack of interactive feedback.
*   **Conversion Paths**: Anonymous Visitor -> Click "MyBratha" -> Asks question -> Subscribes to Newsletter or navigates to `/tools/calculator` -> Submits email.
*   **Desired Outcomes**: Entertained, gains a basic understanding of the brand's luxury offerings, and remembers the name "Sarkin Mota".

---

## 2. High-Net-Worth Buyer (The VIP Client)
*   **Goal**: Secure a rare vehicle (e.g., Xiaomi YU7, Cybertruck) with verified customs duty paid in Abuja/Lagos.
*   **Entry Points**: Direct typing of URL, referral from supercar clubs, search engine queries.
*   **Actions**:
    *   Navigate directly to `/cars/executive`, `/cars/sport`, or `/cars/suvs`.
    *   Filter catalog for specific models, years, or duty status.
    *   Examine vehicle specifications and history check page to verify the car is accident-free.
    *   Use the VIP Concierge service (`/network/concierge`) to order a custom configuration.
*   **Pain Points**: Anxiety about import fraud, lack of transparent documentation (salvage cars risk), difficulty coordinating secure payment and logistics.
*   **Conversion Paths**: Search vehicle -> View Detail -> Verify VIN history -> Submit lead inquiry form -> Phone consultation with Alamin Sarkinmota.
*   **Desired Outcomes**: Purchase a pristine luxury car with zero legal/customs hassle, secure delivery to Abuja show room.

---

## 3. Private Seller (The Upgrade seeker)
*   **Goal**: Trade in an existing luxury SUV for a newer model (e.g., swapping a GLE 53 for a GLE 63S).
*   **Entry Points**: Clicking "Sell or Swap" in the footer, links from social pages.
*   **Actions**:
    *   Use the Valuation Tool (`/tools/valuation`) to get a baseline price.
    *   Fill out the multi-stage `/sell-swap` upload wizard.
    *   Submit condition logs and upload exterior/interior photos.
*   **Pain Points**: Low-ball offers from local buyers, long waiting lists, uncertainty about the value of their vehicle.
*   **Conversion Paths**: Access `/sell-swap` -> Compute valuation -> Enter vehicle details -> Select swap target -> Submit swap request.
*   **Desired Outcomes**: Receive a fair value quote for their old car and drive away in an upgraded model.

---

## 4. Dealer Partner (The Auto Broker)
*   **Goal**: Reach high-end buyers in Abuja and utilize Sarkin Mota's premium platform brand to sell inventory.
*   **Entry Points**: Partner Network link (`/network/partner`), footer "Careers" or "Dealers".
*   **Actions**:
    *   Register and log in to the Seller Dashboard (`/sell/dashboard`).
    *   Create and upload new listings, inputting VINs and condition specifications.
    *   Monitor lead notifications and reply directly to buyer messages.
*   **Pain Points**: Subscription fees, lack of backend analytics, low lead quality.
*   **Conversion Paths**: Onboard via `/network/partner` -> Subscribe to Dealer plan -> Upload listings -> Close sales from platform leads.
*   **Desired Outcomes**: Streamlined inventory management and consistent lead flows.

---

## 5. Editorial Administrator (The Editor/Author)
*   **Goal**: Write automotive news, reviews, and political campaign press releases.
*   **Entry Points**: Admin CMS Login (`/admin` or `/dashboard`).
*   **Actions**:
    *   Open CMS text editor.
    *   Upload images to media vault.
    *   Tag articles (`#ElectricVehicles`, `#YolaConstituency`) and publish.
*   **Pain Points**: Clunky block editors, lack of drafts autosaving, poor media organization.
*   **Conversion Paths**: Log in -> Write content -> Review and preview draft -> Publish.
*   **Desired Outcomes**: Clean, responsive layout for article publications.

---

## 6. Super Administrator (The Manager)
*   **Goal**: Manage vehicle inventory, oversee leads, monitor dealer registrations, control CMS posts, and check auditing logs.
*   **Entry Points**: Admin back-end login.
*   **Actions**:
    *   Verify dealer registrations and approve car listings.
    *   Assign incoming buyer leads to available sales reps.
    *   Monitor transaction metrics and security settings.
*   **Pain Points**: System overload, unauthorized edits, lack of audit trails, spam inquiries.
*   **Conversion Paths**: Access main admin dashboard -> Review pending items -> Approve listings & resolve leads.
*   **Desired Outcomes**: Full control over platform operations, data integrity, and lead attribution.
