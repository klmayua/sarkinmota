# Complete Taxonomy Directory - Sarkin Mota Autos

This document defines the structured taxonomies, categories, tags, brands, locations, and services that organize the Sarkin Mota Autos platform.

---

## 1. Vehicle Taxonomies

Vehicle listings are classified by Body Type (Vehicle Categories), Brands, Models, and Fuel Type.

### Vehicle Categories (Primary Body Types)
*   **Executive Class**: High-end sedans and limousines (e.g., Mercedes S-Class, BMW 7-Series, Audi A8).
*   **Sport & Performance**: Supercars and sports cars (e.g., Audi R8, Porsche 911).
*   **Premium SUVs**: Luxury off-road and utility vehicles (e.g., Mercedes-AMG GLE 63S, Nissan Patrol).
*   **Electric Vehicles (EVs)**: Pure battery electric vehicles (e.g., Xiaomi YU7, BMW i7, Tesla Cybertruck, Stelato S9).
*   **Daily Drivers**: Premium commuter cars (e.g., Toyota Camry, Honda Accord).
*   **Power Bikes**: High-performance motorcycles.
*   **Executive Buses & Vans**: Luxury passenger vans (e.g., Mercedes Sprinter VIP configurations).
